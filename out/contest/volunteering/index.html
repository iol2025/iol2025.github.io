Volunteering

The IOL would not be able to run smoothly without the help of our amazing volunteers. They help us with the preparation of activities, distribution of materials, escorting and helping the participants, and keeping the overall flow and energy of daily life at IOL.

We are looking for both Taiwanese and international volunteers, who are proactive and happy to work with young people, as well as feel comfortable and excited in a multilingual environment. Good English skills are necessary and any other languages are a great plus!

  • Application Time: 2025 January 13th to February 7th

  • Application Instructions: https://risu.io/PO7Wr

  • Recommendation Letters Instructions: https://risu.io/OYdEY

  • Recommendation Letters Format: https://risu.io/5nJq8
